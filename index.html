Contour Plots:

A contour plot is a graphical method to visualize the 3-D surface by plotting constant Z slices called contours in a 2-D format.

The contour plot is an alternative to a 3-D surface plot

Purpose of contour plot:

The purpose of contour plot is to display the relationship between two independent variables and a dependent variable.

Formation of contour plot:

1.Vertical axis:Independent variable 2

2.Horizontal axix:Independent variable 1

3.Lines:Iso-response values,can be calculated with the help(x,y)

Where it is used?

1.Education

2.Engineering

3.Geography

4.Scientific reserch

5.Meterology

Types of contour plot:

1.Rectangular contour plot:

A projection of 2D-plot in 2D rectangular canvas.It is the most common form of the contour plot.

2.Polar contour plot:

Polar contour plot is plotted by using the polar coordinates r and theta.

3.Ternary contour plot:

Ternary contour plot is used to represent the relationship between 3 explanatory variable.
